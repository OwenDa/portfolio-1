Adopt

Sadly, sighthounds represent a large percentage of the dogs found in shelters and rescue centres in Ireland and elsewhere. Their use in racing and hunting, much of it illegal, means these sweet and affectionate dogs are particularly vulnerable to exploitation and abandonment. Fortunately, they make wonderful pets, and shelters will be only too glad to introduce you to any of the dogs in their care currently available for adoption.

Remember that shelters want the best for every dog, so feel free to raise any concerns you may have and they'll help you decide whether a particular breed or individual dog is right for you.
